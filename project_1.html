70 PERCENT of the Earth is WATER.

2.5 percent of that water is FRESH. Less than 1 percent of that water is ESAILY ACCESSIBLE.

In 2014, 40/50 state water managers

said they expect water shortages in some portion of their state by 2024.

The average household can waste up to 180 gallons of water per week.


You can help by

    1. Turning off the water when you brush you teeth.
    2. Taking shorter showers.
    3. Wash your dishes by hand.
